Summarize with Wikum

With unique Humane Centric approach backed by the state of the art machine learning and artificial intelligence algorithms, community gets complete control over the collaborative summarization process.

Tags

By addition of auto suggested or user generated tags , the summaries can be easily filtered out , or grouped together for further action

Flags

Flags empowers users to moderate the lower quality or malicious summaries they feel are not as per the community guidelines

Edit

Owing to its wiki philosophy , Wikum lets the user edit the existing summaries to make them even more better and of high quality.

History

Wikum keeps a log of all the older summaries and edits which lets the community keep a record of all the activities .

Workspaces

Wikum understand that there are situations when the individuals and organization might want to limit the discussion and summaries for members only, hence we have both the public and private workspaces.
